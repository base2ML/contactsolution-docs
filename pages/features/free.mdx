## Placement Tracking

The core value of ContactSolution - track where your marketing touchpoints are generating engagement.

### How It Works

1. **Create a Campaign** - Group placements by initiative (open house, product launch)
2. **Add Placements** - Create unique QR codes with labels (Yard Sign, Flyer, Table Tent)
3. **Deploy** - Place your QR codes in the real world
4. **Track** - See which placements are driving scans

### Getting Started

1. Go to [seeme.contactsolution.io](https://seeme.contactsolution.io)
2. Sign up with Google, Apple, or email
3. Go to **Dashboard** → **Campaigns**
4. Create a campaign (e.g., "Open House March 2024")
5. Add placements with labels
6. Download and deploy your QR codes

<Callout type="tip">
  Start with your highest-value touchpoints first - yard signs, reception desks, or product displays.
</Callout>

## Digital Card (Placement Utility)

Your card is a utility that enhances placement engagement - when someone scans your QR, they can save your contact info.

### Card Features
- Professional digital business card
- Attached to placements via intermediary pages
- vCard download for easy contact saving
- Apple/Google Wallet passes
- Up to 5 social/website links

### Creating Your Card

1. From dashboard, go to **Create Card**
2. Fill in your details:
   - Name and title
   - Contact information
   - Photo (optional)
   - Social media links
3. Click **Generate Card**

Your card is ready in under 2 minutes.

<Callout type="tip">
  Add a professional photo - cards with photos get 3x more engagement than those without.
</Callout>

## Intermediary Landing Page

For each placement, toggle an intermediary page that shows your card before any redirect:

- Shows your contact card when someone scans
- Custom CTA button text
- Optional auto-redirect countdown
- Great for capturing attention at high-value placements

## Sharing Methods

### QR Code
The primary engagement mechanism:
1. Click **Download QR** for any placement
2. Choose size (recommend 400px for printed materials)
3. Add to your marketing materials

### Direct Link
Copy placement URLs for digital sharing:
- Text message
- Email
- Social media

### Wallet Passes
Add your card to Apple/Google Wallet for quick sharing at events.

## Email Signature

Generate an HTML signature with your card link and QR:
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Email Signature**
2. Choose a style (Minimal, Professional, or Bold)
3. Copy the HTML
4. Paste into Gmail, Outlook, or your email client

## Referral Program

Earn credits by referring others:
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Referrals**
2. Copy your referral link
3. Share with colleagues
4. Earn credits toward Pro tier
