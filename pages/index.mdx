# Welcome to ContactSolution

**Marketing attribution for offline touchpoints.**

ContactSolution helps you track every yard sign, flyer, trade show booth, and QR code. Know exactly which placements drive real engagement.

## Platform Tiers

### [Free](/features/free/)
Perfect for getting started. Create attribution campaigns, deploy QR placements, and see basic engagement counts. Includes contact card landing pages and wallet passes.

### [Pro ($29/mo)](/features/pro/)
Full attribution data for professionals. Unlimited campaigns, detailed analytics (when, where, device), CRM integrations, lead scoring, and follow-up tools.

## Core Concepts

### Placements
A **placement** is any marketing touchpoint you want to trackâ€”a yard sign, flyer, booth banner, email signature, or product packaging. Each placement gets a unique QR code so you know exactly which one drove the engagement.

### Campaigns
Group related placements into **campaigns**. For example, a "Spring Trade Show" campaign might include placements for your booth banner, handout flyer, and badge QR. Compare performance across placements to see what works.

### Engagement
When someone scans your QR code, you see the **engagement**. Free tier shows total counts. Pro tier shows when they scanned, what device they used, and where they were.
