# Quick Start

Start tracking your marketing placements in under 5 minutes.

import { Callout } from 'nextra/components'

## Step 1: Sign Up

1. Visit [seeme.contactsolution.io](https://seeme.contactsolution.io)
2. Click **"Get Started Free"**
3. Sign in with Google or create an account with email

## Step 2: Create Your First Campaign

After signing in, set up your first placement tracking campaign:

1. Go to **Dashboard** â†’ **Campaigns**
2. Click **Create Campaign**
3. Name your campaign (e.g., "Open House - 123 Main St")
4. Add placements with labels (e.g., "Yard Sign", "Flyer")
5. Download your unique QR codes

<Callout type="tip">
  Start with your highest-value placements first - the ones you most want to track.
</Callout>

## Step 3: Deploy Your QR Codes

Print and place your QR codes in the real world:

- **Yard Signs** - Real estate, political campaigns
- **Table Tents** - Restaurants, retail counters
- **Flyers** - Event handouts, mailers
- **Business Cards** - Networking events
- **Receipts** - Retail tracking

## Step 4: Track Engagement

Check your dashboard to see scan activity:

- **Total Scans** - How many times each placement was scanned
- **Campaign Overview** - Compare placement performance
- **Engagement Feed** - Recent scan activity

## Optional: Add a Contact Card

Enhance your placements with a digital contact card that appears when someone scans:

1. From dashboard, go to **Create Card**
2. Add your name, title, and contact info
3. Upload a photo (optional but recommended)
4. Enable "Show Card" on your placements

<Callout type="info">
  Your card is a utility - it helps convert placement scans into saved contacts.
</Callout>
