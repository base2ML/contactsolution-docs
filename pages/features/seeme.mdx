## Creating Your Card

1. Go to [seeme.contactsolution.io](https://seeme.contactsolution.io)
2. Sign up with Google, Apple, or email
3. Fill in your details:
   - Name and title
   - Contact information
   - Photo (optional)
   - Social media links
4. Click **Generate Card**

Your card is ready to share in under 2 minutes.

<Callout type="tip">
  Add a professional photo - cards with photos get 3x more engagement than those without.
</Callout>

## Sharing Your Card

### QR Code
The most popular way to share. From your dashboard:
1. Click **Download QR**
2. Choose size (recommend 400px for business cards)
3. Add to your materials: business cards, presentations, email signatures

### Direct Link
Copy your card URL and share via:
- Text message
- Email
- Social media
- Anywhere you can paste a link

### Wallet Passes
Add your card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet:
1. View your card
2. Click **Add to Wallet**
3. Choose Apple Wallet or Google Wallet
4. The pass saves to your phone

Now your contacts can tap your phone to view your card instantly.

### Email Signature
Generate an HTML signature with your card link and mini QR code:
1. Go to **Dashboard** and **Email Signature**
2. Choose a style (Minimal, Professional, or Bold)
3. Copy the HTML
4. Paste into Gmail, Outlook, or your email client

## Attribution Campaigns (New!)

Even with the free tier, you can track your physical marketing:

### What You Get
- **3 Campaigns** - Create up to 3 attribution campaigns
- **5 Placements Each** - Add up to 5 unique QR codes per campaign
- **Total Click Counts** - See how many people scanned each campaign
- **Intermediary Pages** - Optional landing page with your card

### How It Works

1. Go to **Dashboard** and **Campaigns**
2. Create a campaign (e.g., "Open House March 2024")
3. Add placements with labels (e.g., "Yard Sign", "Flyer")
4. Download the unique QR codes
5. Place them in the real world
6. Check total scans in your dashboard

<Callout type="info">
  Free tier shows total click counts per campaign. [Upgrade to XraySpecs](/features/xrayspecs) for detailed analytics including when, where, and on what device people scanned.
</Callout>

### Intermediary Landing Page

For each placement, you can toggle an intermediary page:
- Shows your SeeMe card before redirecting
- Custom CTA button text
- Optional auto-redirect countdown
- Great for capturing attention at high-value placements

[Full Campaign Guide](/how-to/campaigns)

## Wallet Passes

Your card can live in Apple Wallet or Google Wallet:

### Benefits
- Always accessible on your phone
- Quick sharing at events
- Works offline
- Professional appearance

### How to Add
1. Open your card
2. Click **Add to Wallet**
3. Choose Apple or Google
4. Confirm the download

The pass includes your QR code, contact info, and a link to your full card.

## Social Links

Add up to 5 social media or website links:
- LinkedIn
- Twitter/X
- Instagram
- GitHub
- Personal website
- Any custom URL

Links appear as icons on your card and are tracked when clicked.

## Email Signature

Generate a professional email signature:

### Styles Available
- **Minimal** - Clean, text-focused
- **Professional** - Includes photo and QR code
- **Bold** - Colorful with prominent social links

### Installation
1. Generate your signature
2. Copy the HTML
3. Go to email settings:
   - **Gmail**: Settings → See all settings → Signature
   - **Outlook**: Settings → Mail → Compose and reply → Email signature
   - **Apple Mail**: Preferences → Signatures

## Referral Program

Earn credits by referring others:

1. Go to **Dashboard** and **Referrals**
2. Copy your referral link
3. Share with friends and colleagues
4. When they sign up, you both get benefits

Referral credits can be applied toward XraySpecs upgrades.
