## Attribution Campaigns

**The core XraySpecs feature** - track your physical marketing with unique QR codes for every placement.

### How It Works

1. **Create a Campaign** - Group QR codes by initiative (property listing, product launch, event)
2. **Add Placements** - Create unique QR codes with labels (Bus Shelter A, Table 5, Flyer Stack)
3. **Deploy** - Print and place your QR codes in the real world
4. **Track** - See exactly which placements drive engagement

### Intermediary Landing Page

Toggle an optional landing page that appears before redirecting:

- **Show Your SeeMe Card** - Attach your digital business card to any QR placement
- **Custom CTA** - Personalize the continue button text
- **Auto-redirect** - Optional countdown timer (0-10 seconds)

Perfect for realtors, marketers, and retailers who want to capture contact info while tracking attribution.

<Callout type="tip">

## Webhooks & Automations

Build powerful automations that trigger when someone interacts with your card:

### Supported Events

| Event | Trigger |
|-------|---------|
| **Lead Captured** | Someone submits the contact form |
| **Card Viewed** | Someone views your card |
| **vCard Downloaded** | Someone saves your contact |
| **Wallet Added** | Someone adds card to Apple/Google Wallet |
| **Link Clicked** | Someone clicks a social link |
| **QR Scanned** | Someone scans a campaign QR code |

### Lead Capture Automation

The most powerful event: instantly route leads to your sales tools.

```json
{
  "event": "lead_captured",
  "data": {
    "lead": {
      "name": "Jane Doe",
      "email": "jane@company.com",
      "company": "Acme Corp",
      "message": "Interested in partnership"
    },
    "card": {
      "id": "card_abc123",
      "name": "John Smith - Sales"
    }
  }
}
```
