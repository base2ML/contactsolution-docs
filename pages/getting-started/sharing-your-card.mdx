# Card Sharing Options

Ways to share your digital contact card directly or through placements.

import { Callout } from 'nextra/components'

<Callout type="info">
  For placement tracking (the core ContactSolution feature), see the [Campaign Guide](/how-to/campaigns). This page covers direct card sharing.

## QR Code

Your card has a unique QR code that anyone can scan.

### Display on Phone
1. Open your dashboard
2. Your QR code is displayed prominently
3. Have someone scan it with their phone camera

### Download QR Code
1. Click **"Download QR"** on your dashboard
2. Choose format: PNG (for screens) or SVG (for print)
3. Use in presentations, badges, or printed materials

<Callout type="tip">
  For tracked QR codes, use Campaign placements instead. Direct card QR codes don't provide detailed engagement analytics.
</Callout>

## Direct Link

Every card has a unique shareable URL.

### Copy Link
1. Click **"Copy Link"** on your dashboard
2. Paste anywhere: texts, emails, social media

### Your Card URL Format
```
https://seeme.contactsolution.io/card/{your-card-id}
```

## Email Signature

Add your card to your email signature for automatic sharing.

2. Choose a style: Minimal, Professional, or Bold
3. Copy the HTML code
4. Paste into your email client's signature settings

### Supported Email Clients
- Gmail
- Outlook
- Apple Mail
- Most corporate email systems

## Wallet Passes

Add your card to digital wallets for easy access.

### Apple Wallet
1. Click **"Add to Apple Wallet"**
2. Tap **"Add"** when prompted
3. Find your card in the Wallet app

### Google Wallet
1. Click **"Add to Google Wallet"**
2. Sign in if prompted
3. Card appears in Google Wallet

<Callout type="info">
  Wallet passes update automatically when you change your card details.
</Callout>

## vCard Download

Let contacts save you directly to their phone contacts.

1. Click **"Download vCard"** on your dashboard
2. Share the .vcf file
3. Recipients tap to add you to their contacts

## Sharing vs Placement Tracking

| Method | Use Case | Analytics |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| **Direct Card Sharing** | Quick contact exchange | Basic view counts |
| **Campaign Placements** | Marketing attribution | Full engagement analytics |

For marketing attribution (yard signs, flyers, event materials), use Campaign placements to get detailed analytics on when, where, and how people engage.

Learn more in the [Campaign Guide](/how-to/campaigns).
