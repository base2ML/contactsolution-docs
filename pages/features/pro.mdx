## Placement Tracking

**The core Pro feature** - unlimited placement tracking with detailed engagement analytics.

### How It Works

1. **Create a Campaign** - Group placements by initiative (property listing, product launch, event)
2. **Add Placements** - Create unique QR codes with labels (Bus Shelter A, Table 5, Flyer Stack)
3. **Deploy** - Print and place your QR codes in the real world
4. **Track** - See exactly which placements drive engagement

### Lead Capture Automation

The most powerful event: instantly route leads to your sales tools.
