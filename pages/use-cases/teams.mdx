Use ContactSolution for your team's digital business cards and marketing attribution.

## Team Use Cases

### Sales Teams

Each sales rep can create their own card with Pro to:
- Track personal networking engagement
- Use attribution campaigns for territory marketing
- Score and follow up on leads
- Integrate with team CRM

**Campaign Example:**
- Rep creates "North Territory Q1" campaign
- Placements: Trade show booth, local events, prospect mailers
- Track which activities generate the most leads

### Marketing Teams

Use attribution campaigns to prove offline marketing ROI:
- Create campaigns for each marketing initiative
- Unique QR codes per placement (billboards, print ads, etc.)
- Full analytics with Pro (when, where, device)
- Export data to marketing dashboards

### Real Estate Teams

Each agent manages their own listings:
- One campaign per property
- Placements: Yard sign, flyer box, open house, mailer
- See which placements drive the most inquiries
- Intermediary pages show agent's contact card

<Callout type="tip">
  Free tier users get 3 campaigns with 5 placements each - perfect for testing the system before upgrading.
</Callout>

## Team Onboarding

### Getting Started

1. Each team member signs up at seeme.contactsolution.io
2. Creates their digital business card
3. Upgrades to Pro for full analytics (optional)
4. Creates campaigns for their marketing activities

### Recommended Setup

**For Each Team Member:**
- Professional profile photo
- Consistent job title format
- Company website link
- LinkedIn profile link

**For Team Campaigns:**
- Naming convention: "[Name] - [Campaign] - [Quarter]"
- Standard placement labels across team
- Shared tracking spreadsheet for comparisons

## Attribution Campaigns for Teams

### Coordinated Campaigns

Teams can run coordinated campaigns while each member tracks their individual contribution:

**Example: Trade Show Booth**
- Team campaign: "CES 2026 Booth"
- Each rep creates placements with their name
- Compare: "Banner - Alex" vs "Banner - Jordan"
- See which rep's placement drives more engagement

### Territory Tracking

Sales teams covering different regions:
- Create campaigns per territory
- Placements match local marketing activities
- Compare territory performance
- Optimize marketing spend by region

## Analytics & Reporting

Export and combine data for team reporting:

2. Combine in shared dashboard

## Best Practices

### Naming Conventions

Consistent naming helps team reporting:

**Campaigns:**
- `[Product] [Territory] [Quarter]` - "Widget Pro East Q1"
- `[Event] [Year]` - "CES 2026"
- `[Property] [City]` - "123 Main St Austin"

**Placements:**
- Descriptive location: "Front Window Display"
- Include date if relevant: "Jan Mailer"
- Team member prefix if needed: "Alex - Booth Banner"

### Regular Review

- Weekly: Check campaign performance
- Monthly: Compare placement effectiveness
- Quarterly: Optimize marketing mix

### Team Coordination

- Share campaign insights in team meetings
- Document what works in shared playbook
- Test different placements across team members
