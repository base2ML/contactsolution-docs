# Team Settings

Manage your team members, roles, and collaborative features.

import { Callout } from 'nextra/components'

<Callout type="info">
  Team features are available on Pro tier for organizations.
</Callout>

## Overview

Team Settings allow you to manage multiple users under a single organization account. Control permissions, share cards across the team, and maintain consistent branding across all team members.

## Team Dashboard

Access team settings at **Dashboard** → **Team** → **Settings**. The dashboard shows:

- Team member list
- Role assignments
- Invitation status
- Activity overview

## Team Roles

### Admin
Full access to all team features:
- Manage team members
- Configure billing
- Set organization branding
- Access all analytics
- Manage integrations

### Manager
Team oversight capabilities:
- Invite new members
- View team analytics
- Manage card templates
- Cannot access billing

### Member
Standard team member:
- Create personal cards
- Use team templates
- View own analytics
- Cannot manage team

## Inviting Team Members

1. Go to **Team** → **Settings**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter email address(es)
4. Select role
5. Click **Send Invitation**

Invitations expire after 7 days. Pending invitations appear in the team list.

## Managing Members

### Edit Member
- Click the menu icon on a member row
- Select **Edit**
- Change role or permissions
- Click **Save**

### Remove Member
- Click the menu icon
- Select **Remove**
- Confirm removal
- Member loses access immediately

<Callout type="warning">
  Removing a member does not delete their cards. Cards can be transferred or archived.
</Callout>

## Organization Branding

### Brand Settings
Configure organization-wide branding:

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Logo** | Displayed on all team cards |
| **Primary Color** | Brand color for cards |
| **Company Name** | Organization display name |
| **Domain** | Custom URL (Enterprise plans) |

### Card Templates
Create standardized templates for the team:

1. Design a card with your branding
2. Click **Save as Template**
3. Enable for team use
4. Members can use as starting point

## Team Analytics

View aggregated team metrics:

- Total cards created
- Combined scan count
- Top performing cards
- Team activity timeline

### Export Team Data
Download team analytics:
- CSV export
- Date range selection
- Filter by member or card

## Single Sign-On (SSO)

<Callout type="info">
  SSO is available on Enterprise plans. Contact sales for details.
</Callout>

Configure SSO for your organization:

1. Go to **Team** → **Settings** → **SSO**
2. Select your identity provider
3. Enter SSO configuration details
4. Test connection
5. Enable for team

Supported providers:
- Okta
- Azure AD
- Google Workspace
- SAML 2.0 generic

## Billing & Seats

### Seat Management
- View current seat usage
- Add/remove seats
- Per-seat billing on Pro tier

### Upgrading
To add more team features:
1. Go to **Team** → **Settings** → **Billing**
2. View current plan
3. Click **Upgrade**
4. Select new plan

### Two-Factor Authentication

- Reset member 2FA if needed

### Session Management
- View active sessions
- Force logout for members
- Set session timeout

### Audit Log
Track team activity:
- Member logins
