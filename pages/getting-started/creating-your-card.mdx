# Card Utility Setup

Set up your digital contact card - a utility that enhances placement engagement by letting scanners save your contact info.

## Card Fields

### Required Fields

| Field | Description | Tips |
|-------|-------------|------|
| **Display Name** | Your name as shown on the card | Use your professional name |
| **Email** | Primary contact email | Use a professional email |

### Recommended Fields

| Field | Description | Impact |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| **Job Title** | Your current role | Helps people remember you |
| **Company** | Your organization | Adds credibility |
| **Phone** | Direct contact number | Optional but useful |
| **Profile Photo** | Your headshot | 3x more engagement with photos |

### Optional Fields

- **LinkedIn URL** - Link to your professional profile
- **Twitter/X** - Your social handle
- **Website** - Personal or company site
- **Bio** - Brief description (max 160 characters)

## Adding Social Links

You can add up to 5 social links on Free tier, unlimited on Pro.

1. Go to your card editor
2. Scroll to **"Social Links"**
3. Click **"Add Link"**
4. Select the platform (LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, etc.)
5. Paste your profile URL

<Callout type="tip">
  Social links appear as icons on your card. The most popular platforms are recognized automatically.
</Callout>

## Profile Photo Best Practices

For the best appearance on your card and wallet passes:

- **Size**: At least 400x400 pixels
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Style**: Professional headshot, good lighting
- **Background**: Solid or simple backgrounds work best

## Card Preview

As you edit, you'll see a live preview of how your card looks:

- **Mobile view** - How it appears on phones
- **QR code** - Your unique scannable code
- **Wallet preview** - How it looks in Apple/Google Wallet
