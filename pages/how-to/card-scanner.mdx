## Overview

The Card Scanner uses AI-powered OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract contact information from physical business cards. Simply snap a photo and let the system automatically parse names, emails, phone numbers, and more.

## Scan Quality Tips

For best results:

- **Good lighting** - Avoid shadows on the card
- **Flat surface** - Keep the card level, not curved
- **High contrast** - Light cards on dark backgrounds work best
- **Focus** - Ensure text is sharp, not blurry
- **Full card** - Include all edges in the frame

## Editing Scanned Information

After scanning:

1. Review the extracted fields
2. Click any field to edit
3. Add missing information manually
4. Click **Save Contact** when ready

## Actions After Scanning

Once saved, you can:

- **Add to connections** - Save to your contacts
- **Send follow-up** - Trigger an automated campaign
- **Export** - Download as vCard or CSV
- **Create card** - Generate a digital card for them

## Batch Scanning

For multiple cards:

1. Enable **Batch Mode** in scanner settings
2. Scan cards in succession
3. Review all at once in the queue
4. Approve or edit before saving

## Privacy & Storage

<Callout type="warning">
  Scanned images are processed locally when possible. Images are not stored after OCR extraction.
</Callout>

- Card images are not retained after processing
- Extracted data is encrypted at rest
- You control data retention in Settings > Privacy
