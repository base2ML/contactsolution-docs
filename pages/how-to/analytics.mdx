## Understanding Metrics

### Total Scans
The complete count of times your placements have been scanned, including repeat visitors.

### Unique Visitors
The number of distinct people who have engaged, based on device fingerprinting.

### Device Breakdown
- **Mobile** - Smartphone views (typically from QR scans)
- **Desktop** - Computer views (typically from email links)
- **Tablet** - Tablet device views

### Geographic Data
See where your engagement is coming from:
- Country
- City (when available)
- Helps identify which markets respond to your marketing

### Time Analytics
Understand when your placements get the most engagement:
- Hour of day breakdown
- Day of week patterns
- Useful for timing follow-ups

### Source Tracking
See how people found your placements:
- **QR Code** - Scanned at locations
- **Direct Link** - Clicked from text/email
- **Email Signature** - From your email footer
- **Wallet Pass** - Opened from Apple/Google Wallet

## Using Analytics Effectively

### After Events
Check analytics the day after events to see:
- How many people actually scanned your QR codes
- Peak engagement times
- Geographic spread of contacts

### For Email Campaigns
Use email signature analytics to understand:
- Which emails generate engagement
- Open and engagement rates by recipient type

### For Follow-ups
The best time to follow up is typically:
- Within 24 hours of engagement
- During the viewer's peak engagement times

## Exporting Analytics Data

**Pro users** can export analytics:

1. Go to **Dashboard** ‚Üí **Analytics**
2. Click **Export** button
3. Choose what to export:
   - **Scored Viewers** - Anonymous visitors with engagement scores
   - **Captured Leads** - Contacts who submitted their information
   - **All** - Both types in a unified export
4. Select format:
   - **CSV** - For spreadsheets (Excel, Google Sheets)
   - **JSON** - For developers and integrations
5. Choose which fields to include
6. Click Export to download

### Export Fields

**Scored Viewers:**
- Viewer ID, Score, Temperature
- Last Activity, Event Count
- Signals, Velocity, Device Type
- Location, First Seen, Recommendation

**Captured Leads:**
- Name, Email, Phone
- Company, Job Title
- Notes, Source, Status, Created Date

## Privacy & Data

- No personal data is collected from viewers without consent
- Geographic data uses IP-based estimation
- Device information uses anonymous fingerprinting
- You can disable analytics collection in settings

<Callout type="warning">
  Analytics data is retained for 12 months. Export data you want to keep long-term.
</Callout>
